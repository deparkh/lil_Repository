Adam Khoo: «Grab the chances when they come because they don't come again»

Adam Khoo, famous trader and millionaire, on the TV show «Hello Singapore» shares his advice about money.

PUBLISHED FEB 05, 2020, 8:00 AM SGT


Lorna Tan   Invest Editor/Senior Correspondent

"It is a pity that you cannot stop time and put everything back to correct previous mistakes. Life does not give a second chance. Therefore live your heart. Trust your heart. Do everything to achieve your goal. Never give up.” - Heath Ledger

Every day we see a small, and sometimes very good chance. This may concern a career, relationship, dream, or life in general.

A stranger smiles at us on the street, friends pull on a walk to the city, a neighbor pulls on a ride on bicycles, a comrade calls to go on a trip, a friend calls to the beach, a classmate invites to work at his firm.

There can be so many possibilities behind these invitations of fate, but we are used to say “no” to new chances. Although these gifts of fate can fundamentally change our lives.

We have all become too lazy, inert and cowardly. We do not grab the opportunities provided, but dismiss them. We control our lives too much, that a step to the side is perceived as a disaster. We have all become conformists. We passively accept the existing order of things and opinions, and sweep away everything new. We have become pensioners and bores ahead of time.

Now, in connection with the current situation, the Ministry of Health recommends not to leave your home without special need and avoid events with a large crowd of people.

Therefore, Adam Khoo wrote some tips for our readers. Read below:
